Overview

Exotica One32 is a premium mixed-use commercial development located on the bustling Noida–Greater Noida Expressway in Sector 132, Noida. This landmark destination seamlessly blends modern architecture with versatile functionality, featuring retail shops, office spaces, business suites, and food court units, all under one roof. Designed for high visibility and built for performance, One32 boasts double-height lobbies, high-speed elevators, and efficient infrastructure tailored to today's business needs. Positioned amidst major residential and institutional hubs, it offers unparalleled accessibility and investment potential.

Amenities

Experience unmatched convenience and lifestyle amenities at One32, including landscaped sit-outs, a gym, yoga and meditation zones, an amphitheatre, EV charging, high-speed elevators, multi-level parking, and round-the-clock security, all curated to elevate work and leisure in one integrated space.
